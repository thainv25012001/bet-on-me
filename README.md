BetOnMe – Bet on Your Own Discipline

BetOnMe is an execution-focused productivity app designed to turn your goals into real, daily action.

Most people don’t fail because they lack knowledge — they fail because they lack consistent execution. BetOnMe solves this by combining AI planning, behavioral psychology, and financial commitment to help users stay accountable and follow through on their goals.

Users start by defining a goal they want to achieve within a specific timeframe — whether it’s learning a new skill, improving their health, preparing for an exam, or building a project. BetOnMe’s AI then breaks that goal down into a clear, structured plan with small, actionable daily tasks.

To activate the plan, users commit by placing a small daily stake. This financial commitment creates a powerful accountability mechanism: when users complete their daily tasks, they keep their stake and maintain their progress streak. If they fail to complete the tasks, they lose that day’s stake.

This system leverages the psychological principle of loss aversion, making users far more likely to follow through on their commitments.

BetOnMe also gamifies the experience by tracking progress, streaks, and milestones, helping users build consistency and momentum over time.

By combining goal planning, accountability, and gamification, BetOnMe transforms good intentions into consistent action.

Bet on yourself. Execute daily. Win your goals.
